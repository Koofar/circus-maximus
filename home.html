The Circus Maximus

The Circus Maximus was the place for all events to take place. A publice place for events, meant for chariot racing mainly. The latin name is "Circus Maximus" but in english is the same. Located in rome, made of stone, it was constructed in the 6th century BCE. This place was constructed when Lucius Tarquinius Priscus was in power
